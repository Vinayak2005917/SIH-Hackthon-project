About Us

Your hackathon project's website features an innovative "paths system" designed to enhance interaction between students and teachers. This system allows teachers to create structured, timeline-like paths, where each path is divided into various segments or "pieces." These pieces could represent lessons, activities, or milestones that guide students through the learning process. Students can interact with these pieces, which may include tasks, quizzes, or multimedia content, making the learning experience more dynamic and engaging. The paths system provides a clear, visual roadmap of the educational journey, fostering a more interactive and personalized learning environment.
